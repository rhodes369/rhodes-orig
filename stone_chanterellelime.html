Stone

Chanterelle Limestone

Description

Deep in the primitive forest where wild mushrooms and mythical wood sprites dance amid mossy hollows, Chanterelle Limestone rises out of the soft earth. The warm tones of this high-density limestone seem saturated with tannins from the forest fl oor. RAS hand texturing homogenizes and subtly blends any stone-to-stone variations, while the fi nal honed and polished fi nishes highlight darker fossil fragments, making them fl oat as in a wild mushroom bisque. Like its sister stone Pearl, Chanterelle Limestone off ers a rare surface translucence, catching warm light and holding it, lending the surface a soft luminescence. Because it lacks the crystalline structure that holds the sun's heat â€“ or burns one's feet like granite or sandstone or concrete, Chanterelle Limestone is the perfect choice for pool decks. Its more saturated color relaxes the eye, particularly in outdoor applications, making it ideal for any exterior hardscape bathed in strong sunlight. Visually arresting in both modern and classically-modeled designs, this stone displays a timeless beauty. Like all hard and lighter-colored limestones, Chanterelle needs to be mortar set in white Portland cement. To avoid edge chipping on veneer, RAS recommends raked or wedge mortar method setting technique.
