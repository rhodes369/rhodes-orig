Who We Are

The mission of Rhodes Architectural Stone is to delight our customers and realize their architectural dreams in exquisite antique and newly crafted natural stone. Founded by master mason and principal entrepreneur Richard Rhodes, the company and its products are informed by Rhodes' formal apprenticeship in Siena, Italy, and 20 years of experience in stone art, quarrying, installation and fabrication. RAS focuses on large, multi-year projects, where highest quality, craftsmanship and design vision are required.

From its headquarters in Seattle, RAS manages a global stone production team using state-of-the-art communications. Combining hand techniques of stonework in the tradition of renaissance Europe with 21st century information technology, RAS works to fulfill the visions of clients throughout North America.

What We Do

Rhodes Architectural Stone is dedicated to helping our customers realize their architectural dreams in exquisite, newly hand-crafted and antique natural stone. With an extensive history of work in stone design, fabrication, and installation, we showcase the highest and best use of stone and focus on projects that require creative vision, striking design, and master craftsmanship. Natural stone solutions provided by Rhodes Architectural Stone incorporate the finest granites, limestones, and sandstones available in the world. Employing techniques and tools in the tradition of renaissance Europe, we create hand-crafted finishes for our renowned collection of newly quarried materials. We also offer antique materials with unique patinas, colors, and textures from centuries of wear. By reclaiming these materials, we are able to preserve part of the world's patrimony that would otherwise be lost due to urbanization of rural areas and large-scale public works projects.

How We Work

View examples of mock-up photos

From idea to reality, every project large or small is supported by our full set of services. RAS sales consultants meet with customers to discuss design ideas. We listen, understand your needs, and collaborate in project development, drawing upon our database of over 35,000 images of installed and in situ stonework. For each customer, we create a confidential website to facilitate communication of digital images and design dialogue to move projects from development to execution in a timely manner.

Mockups and Stone Selection
Mockups play an important role early on to test designs and make stone decisions. We quickly produce mockups to help our customers visualize final stone scale, material, and finish. Images of mockups are posted to the website for client review and approval. If appropriate, we ship approved mockups to project sites to serve as an installation guide.

Value Engineering and Contracting
We work with design teams to value engineer stone applications to ensure that customers get the most for their money. The contract between the customer and RAS delineates precise quantities and specifications. A fifty-percent deposit finalizes the order. Then, RAS designers provide shop drawings to specify fabrication dimension, finish, and tolerance for every face of every piece of stone.

View examples of inspection photos

Production and Delivery
Stone selection determines country of fabrication. Our global stone production team trains and certifies our nearly two dozen contract factories to produce stone exactly as specified for each project. The team also performs rigorous quality inspections throughout the fabrication process. When appropriate, complex assemblies are pre-assembled and images are posted to the customer website for review and approval. We manage crating, customs clearances, and handling logistics to ship the order from country of origin directly to your job site. Production and transportation progress are continuously monitored to keep our customers abreast of order status.

Why Customers Choose Us
With our exquisite materials and artisan craftsmanship, the professional team at RAS works to translate your architectural legacy into the highest quality building stone.

Shop Drawing Process
RAS shop drawing process is extensive and considered a model in the industry. For all bounding blocks, assembled architectural carved objects, the process is composed of two distinct steps: context/assembly drawings (showing exact location and overall dimensions, piece numbering and logistical sequencing,) and piece drawings (showing exact object dimensions and detail).

Bios

Richard Rhodes, Founder

Richard is a founding partner of Rhodes Architectural Stone, and a nationally recognized entrepreneur, artist, and designer with almost two decades of experience in the stone industry. He began his masonry career with a formal, hands-on apprenticeship in Siena, Italy. In 1984, he founded Rhodes Masonry, Inc., one of RAS's principal affiliated companies. Richard has received many honors for his entrepreneurial achievements, including the SBA Small Business Administration HERO award for outstanding small business achievement. He is past president of the YEO Seattle Chapter (Young Entrepreneurs Organization), served on the International board of YEO as the VP of Strategic Relationships, and is past chairman of the YEO Advisory Council for the Kauffman Center for Entrepreneurial Leadership, the country's twelfth largest philanthropic organization. Richard is a graduate of the 2001 Birthing of Giants Class at MIT for High Growth Entrepreneurs and is currently a member of the Young Presidents Organization (YPO). He and his work have been featured in publications such as Architectural Digest, Newsweek, and Sunset Magazine. In addition, Martha Stewart has selected the company for a television show highlighting the Best of the Northwest.

Richard has completed several major sculptural commissions. Although most of his work is privately held, his largest public sculpture is on permanent display at the newly opened Tacoma Art Museum, designed by Antoine Predock Architects.

 

Christopher D. Strachan, CEO

Chris Strachan brings to RAS a breadth of leadership experience in management, business development, marketing and service. For the past 25 years, Chris has developed, formed and run exciting companies in a wide range of fields. From a Regional VP for a Citi Group Financial company, property development, Chief underwriter for the 5th insurance and surety company in Utah, contract managment and monotoring, aviation services, to a motivational speaker. He has a record of proven leadership from start-ups to mature businesses, with emphasis on meeting client and shareholder expectations. Mr. Strachan particularly enjoys working closely with clients' project and creative teams.
