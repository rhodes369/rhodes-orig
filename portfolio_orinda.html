Orinda Public Library

Project Principals

Architect: Stastny Brun Architects

Contractor: Overaa Construction

Enter the City of Orinda's new library from beyond the dramatic stone waterfall. A tunnel, not shown, leads visitors to the fountain for discovery of the grand stairs. The fountain and landscape walls are covered in a Golden Granite veneer.

The grand stairs lead visitors to the upper plaza which includes a small pool at the top of the fountain and a view of the main plaza below.

A view of the rear of the building shows the landscaping and a fenced reading area. Visitors may enjoy outdoor reading prior to leaving the library. An attached auditorium is located to the right.
