Turner Residence

Project Principals

Architecture: Nievera Williams Design, Inc.

Perched over Silicon Valley, this estate incorporates both formal dimensional stone in the 18th and 19th century tradition with more rustic site walls and outbuildings. The extensive site is terraced with stone and planted with vines.

The wrought stone elements are crisply executed in Silk Road Sandstone with a sand-rubbed finish. This hand-carved architectural work was created using a stereotomy templating technique perfected by French craftsman in the early Renaissance. The work is extremely technical and represents some of the best craftsmanship in the company's history.

The outbuildings and site walls are executed in heavily textured Amandine Limestone in a blend of 12th century hand textures; pineapple and split-face. The rougher surface catches shadow and light, glowing in the warm California light. The company's 500 year old Antique Salmon Granite pavements complete the paving on the formal terraces with timeless elegance.
