Stone

Fire Green Granite

Description

Fire Green Granite draws its name from the ancient dragon's breath rumored to have created its deeply saturated color. Geologically unique, the stone performs a visual dance, with the black mica and mineral flecks stippling the surface. The delicate coloration of Fire Green Granite belies its many expansive and versatile applications. Suitable for hard climates with severe cold, able to resist sea spray, heavy foot and auto traffic, this stone is one of our most flexible materials. Whether used for foundational veneer, for paving, or for more exotic showpiece sinks and tubs carved from a single block, Fire Green Granite will not disappoint. Tooling marks from our fine hand finishes expose the deep luster of encased minerals, producing a milky, whitish color to contrast the deeper, unmarked surface. Fire Green Granite takes all textures and hand finishes well and may be installed in any of the standard setting techniques – mortar or thinset on slab or by sand-setting.
