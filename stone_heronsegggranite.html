Stone

Heron's Egg Granite

Description

Like a precious egg cast into stone by time, Heron's Egg Granite captures the best of the geological past and inspires fresh concepts to hatch in the future. In this stone, shimmering mica and feldspar glisten provocatively, beckoning a closer look, while quartz, saturated with deep grays, blankets the surface with a calm richness. The result is one of the most visually stunning materials in our collection. Its appearance is enhanced by our hand finishes, which batter and dampen the surface, their crushing blows creating clean white crystals that contrast the flickering minerals to great effect. Often used as a split-face wall surface or as a paver, the hand-tooled finishes on Heron's Egg Granite will highlight lintels, sills and copings. Honing the surface darkens it even further and produces dramatic slab work. As with many of our materials, large-scale formats are available by special order, allowing this material to be scaled appropriately for larger structures. Heron's Egg Granite may be installed in all standard setting techniques: mortar or thinset on slab or by sand setting.
