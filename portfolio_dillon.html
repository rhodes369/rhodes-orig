Dillon Residence

Project Principals

Architect: Sullivan Conard Architects

Interior Design: Tedrick & Bennett

Landscape Architect: Richard Haag & Associates

Enter the warm chestnut colored antique granite cobblestone drive accented with a Golden Granite, Tudor Weave patterned retaining wall. Pause to take in the beauty of Lake Washington from the Golden Granite and Antique Salmon Granite patio of this newly constructed Cape Cod inspired home.The open floor plan and clean interiors are accented with an array of exotic stones including Antique Salmon Plank and Ankar firebrick fireplace and New Curry Limestone countertops. Harmony is created by the exclusive selection of new and antique natural stone installed throughout the residence.
