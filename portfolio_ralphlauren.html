Ralph Lauren

Project Principals

Architect: Halper Owens Architects

Interior Design: Pamela Pearce Design

Landscape Design: Pamela Rhodes

The once 70's barn-style house with alu-minum windows is just a memory thanks to extensive remodeling. Risen in it's place is a tranquil home loosely keeping with the Arts & Crafts style. New and Antique natural stone and wood lend a simple elegance throughout. Observe the master craftsmanship forever captured in the wall veneer tight joints and key hole view details. Gardens and pathways are given history with antique granite planks, stair treads, mill wheels and antique carved stone objects.
