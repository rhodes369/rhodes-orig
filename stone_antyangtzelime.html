Antique Yangtze Limestone

FINISH: -- Missing Info --

Available finishes: -- Missing Info --

Recommended Uses: veneer, quoins

Antique Yangtze Limestone, a high-density, fossil-rich limestone, is reclaimed from the region of China’s most revered river, the Yangtze. Since construction of the Three Gorges Dam Project across the Yangtze began over a decade ago, an estimated 1,600 villages along the river’s edge have been doomed to be submerged and lost forever. RAS has been able to collect a great store of this exquisite stone from abandoned homes and school yards, making it available for re-use. Antique Yangtze Limestone carries its original textures of point-stalk, pineapple, and split face finishes. Centuries of weathering have developed rich patinas that range in color from toasty beige to sage and charcoal. Richly fossilized, Antique Yangtze Limestone blends seamlessly with New Yangtze Limestone in any application. Principle setting methods include our proprietary wedge anchor setting for dry stack applications or clipped and back-filled veneer systems.
